About Michael Kors

Michael Kors(Capri Holdings Limited) is a multinational fashion holding company that was incorporated in the British Virgin Islands. It has executive offices in London and operational offices in New York. The American designer Michael Kors established this company in the year 1981. This company sells clothes, shoes, watches, handbags, and other accessories for men and women. Its handbags have always been a popular trend among women of all ages. In 2015, it had more than 550 stores and over 1500 in-store boutiques in various countries...

Michael Kors watch designs vary to suit different tastes, from classic watches in neutral colors, to bold watches decorated with precious stones or innovative designs. Each watch is not just a tool for measuring time, but an expression of personality and elegance.
